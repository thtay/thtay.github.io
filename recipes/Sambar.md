# Sambar 
Indian sambar to be used in dosa or other dishes

## Ingredients

* Oil
* Mustard Seeds
* Feugreek Seeds
* Red Chili (Dry)
* Curry leaves
* Devil Dung (Aseofetida)
* 1 cut onion
* Tomato
* Some veggies of your choice (carrot, zuchini, etc)
* Salt
* Chili powder
* Sambar powder
* Tamarind water
* Water
* Jagary (sugar)
* Lentil
