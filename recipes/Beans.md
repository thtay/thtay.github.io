# Beans for Rice and Beans
Bean recipe to be used in rice and beans

## Ingredients

* Beans
* 1/2 Onion 
* Garlic
* Chili Powder
* Cumin
* Cilantro
* Worestershire Sauce
* Soy Sauce
* Hot Sauce
* Ketcup
* Tomato


## Steps
1. Cook Onion then add spices and finally pre-cooked beans
